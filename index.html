  • a [resource center] and [automation cooperative] devoted to streamlining the process of creating an internationally-recognized cooperative legal entity
  • an identity system for discovery of cooperatives and their members
  • a marketplace where cooperatives can be connected to financial lenders around the globe in order to issue sophisticated [debt instruments]
